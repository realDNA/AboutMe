———Web Crawler———

I want to do this project in the begining is because my collegues sometimes forgot to order bento dinners which are offered by our company. Due to this reason, i came up with an idea that i could write a simple code to continuously send e-mail to remind them to order bento dinners. At first, there was just a text which said: "Don't forget to order bento dinners", but I felt it was so boring if there was just a section of text. Therefore, features like weather predicion was added, and then articles scrapped from ptt(the biggest forum in taiwan dicussing everything accroding to topics) also be added and then articles are sorted by the response numbers(also can be refered to the degree of popularity).
